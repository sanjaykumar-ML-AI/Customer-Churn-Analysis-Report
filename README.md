# Customer-Churn-Analysis-Report
This Power BI Customer Churn Analysis Report gives insights about parameters which are contributing for customers churn which in terms leading for drecrease in Overall Revenue Generation for Telecom Business 



**Problem Statement**

Customer churn is a major challenge in the telecom industry, directly impacting revenue, customer retention, and business growth. This project analyzes customer behavior, service usage patterns, and churn trends to identify the key factors driving customer attrition and improve retention strategies.


**Project Summary**

Developed a Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard in Power BI to analyze churn behavior across demographics, contract types, internet services, payment methods, tenure, and value-added services. The dashboard provides business insights into customer retention patterns, churn risk factors, and revenue impact.


**Key Challenges Faced**

* Creating accurate churn KPIs and retention metrics
* Analyzing multiple customer attributes impacting churn behavior
* Building meaningful visual storytelling for churn analysis
* Managing relationships between demographic, billing, and service datasets


**Business Recommendations**


* Focus retention campaigns on month-to-month contract customers with high churn rates
* Improve customer engagement for fiber optic users experiencing higher churn
* Promote value-added services like Online Security and Tech Support to reduce churn risk
* Introduce loyalty benefits for long-tenure customers to improve retention
* Optimize pricing and billing strategies for customers with high monthly charges
